Torvy One

The low power IoT dev board for makers.

Connectivity first

Torvy One has a built-in 2.4Ghz radio transceiver with a built-in chip antenna. Not enough for you? External transceivers like HopeRF RFM69HW can be soldered to extend functionalities.

Mesh Networking

Thanks to the built in radio functionalities it's possible to build a mesh network with your favorite technology, whether it's ZigBee, Lightweight Mesh or somethinge else.

Open source

We love open source. Most of our codebase is on GitHub (depending on other vendors licenses) and we support community-powered projects based on our hardware.
